Translate annotated text into emoji 😄

Usage:
  tmoji (Type `Ctrl+D` to Quit)
  tmoji < INPUT.txt > OUTPUT.txt

Rules:
  • @tag attach the emoji after the word (e.g. "@hi" → "hi🙋🏻").
  • :tag replace the tag entirely (e.g. ":wink" → "😉").
  • If a tag has no emoji mapping, the symbol (@ or :) is stripped and the word is preserved.

Example:
  Input: @hi there :! :wink . @wrong :tag
  Output: hi🙋🏻 there ❗️ 😉 . wrong tag

Easily customize emoji mappings in the `Tmoji.txt` file.
